Guess the Emoji!

Guess the emoji is a fun game where players interpret emoji clues to guess words. It challenges creativity and quick thinking, making it great for solo or group play!

How to play?

  1. Choose a Category: Select a category you want (Random 1 word, Movies, Holidays & Events, Places, Video games)
  2. Choose number of rounds: Select a number of round you want (1 round only, 5 rounds, 10 rounds, 15 rounds or 20 rounds)
  3. Start the Game: The game will generate random emojis in 1, 5, 10, 15 or 20 consecutive rounds.
  4. Guess the Word: Look at the emojis and try to figure out what they represent (Hint about the words will always be given)
  5. Submit Your Answer: Type your guess into the input box and submit it.
  6. Check Your Answer: If correct, you win the round! If wrong, you can choose to show the correct answer or try again!
  7. Play Again: Choose a new category and continue guessing new emoji puzzles.
